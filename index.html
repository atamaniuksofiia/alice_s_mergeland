About the Game
Alice's Mergeland is a captivating puzzle adventure set in a magical world inspired by classic fairy tales. Players merge items to discover new objects, solve puzzles, and restore the enchanting lands of Mergeland. Unfold mysteries, interact with whimsical characters, and help Alice on her journey to bring life back to her magical home

Unique Features

  • Expansive World

    Explore diverse lands, from lush meadows to mystical forests, each brimming with secrets

  • Story-Driven Gameplay

    An engaging narrative that unfolds as you merge and progress

  • Magical Artifacts

    Collect and merge unique items with magical properties to advance the story

  • Interactive Characters

    Build relationships with in-game characters, each with their own backstory and quests

  • Evolving Challenges

    Dynamic puzzles that increase in complexity, offering endless hours of fun

  • Customizable Landscape

    Restore Mergeland by designing and personalizing your dreamlike environment
